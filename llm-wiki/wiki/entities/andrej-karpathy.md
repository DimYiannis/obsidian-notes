## Overview

AI researcher and educator. Founding member of OpenAI; former Director of AI at Tesla (Autopilot). Known for high-quality technical education on deep learning and LLMs, including the "Intro to Large Language Models" video and the nanoGPT / llm.c projects.

## Key Facts

- Co-founded OpenAI; later Director of AI at Tesla
- Created llm.c — reproduced GPT-2 training for ~$100–$600 (vs. ~$40k in 2019)
- "Intro to Large Language Models" video: comprehensive general-audience walkthrough of the full LLM training pipeline
- Advocates treating LLMs as tools: "Swiss cheese capabilities — use them but check their work"
