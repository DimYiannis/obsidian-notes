## Overview

AI safety research company, known for the Claude family of models and for foundational work in [[mechanistic-interpretability]]. Its Transformer Circuits thread produced *A Mathematical Framework for Transformer Circuits* (Elhage et al., 2021), which introduced the [[residual-stream]] framing and the discovery of [[induction-heads]].

## Key Facts

- Runs the Transformer Circuits research thread on interpretability.
- Authors of the 2021 mathematical framework reverse-engineering small transformers.
- Founded by researchers including several co-authors of that paper (e.g. Dario Amodei, Chris Olah, Sam McCandlish, Jared Kaplan).
