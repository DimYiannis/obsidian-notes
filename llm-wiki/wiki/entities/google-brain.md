## Overview

Google's deep-learning research group, where *Attention Is All You Need* (Vaswani et al., 2017) was produced — the paper that introduced the [[transformer-architecture]] underlying modern LLMs.

## Key Facts

- Home of the 2017 transformer paper and its eight authors (Vaswani, Shazeer, Parmar, Uszkoreit, Jones, Gomez, Kaiser, Polosukhin).
- The transformer originated in a machine-translation context (WMT 2014 EN→DE / EN→FR).
