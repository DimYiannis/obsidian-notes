# IP address

A label that identifies a device on a network ("Internet Protocol address"). Two jobs: **identity** (who) + **location** (where on the net). Example: `192.168.1.130`.

Stored as a [[bit-binary|32-bit]] number, shown as four [[octet|octets]] in [[dotted-decimal]] form. Splits into a [[network-host-portion|network part and host part]], paired at the hardware level with a [[mac-address]].

Comes in [[ipv4-ipv6|IPv4 and IPv6]] flavours, can be [[public-private|public or private]], and [[static-dynamic|static or dynamic]].
